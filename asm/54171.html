D39B: Reads In-Game Keyboard Controls (1-2-3).
Used by the routine at D392.

D39D IN A,($FE)
D39F BIT 0,A If "1" isn't being pressed then jump to D3A6.
D3A1 JR NZ,$D3A6
D3A3 LD A,$12 Set A=$12 and return.
D3A5 RET

D3A8 JR NZ,$D3AD
