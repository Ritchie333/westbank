D301: Player Left/ Right.
These flags don't control left/ right movement they just indicate whether keys are being pressed/ joystick used.
D301 DEFB $00 Defaults to $00, holds $01 when the player is pressing "right".
D302 DEFB $00 Defaults to $00, holds $01 when the player is pressing "left".
