CE14: Door Routine Buffer.
These buffers point to the routines for character/ door actions for each door that are currently "in-play". Some example routines would be;
Routine Door
D192 "Normal" Bandit
D1DC Bowie
D201 Customer
D241 Julius
D289 "Drawing" Bandit
And will also hold the door closing routines as well.
CE14 DEFW $0000 Door 1 character/ door routine in-play.
CE16 DEFW $0000 Door 2 character/ door routine in-play.
CE18 DEFW $0000 Door 3 character/ door routine in-play.
